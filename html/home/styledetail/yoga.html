Yoga

A group of practices and disciplines incorporating the physical, mental, and spiritual parts of the body. In the western world it commonly denotes the practice of posture based physical fitness, stress relief, and relaxation. Yoga has a long history dating back to 1000 bce. The most common yoga traditions(practices) are Jain, Buddhist, Classical, In Advaita Vedanta, Tantric, Hatha, Laya, and kundalini yoga. Yoga is a versatile fitness which lends itself to being able to be practiced as a stand alone fitness regime, or incorporated into other forms of fitness.
